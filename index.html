Open Source Open Data

A holistic, sustainable approach to government open data.

Mission

OSOD brings open source awareness to the open data community and helps educate all stakeholders on the importance of open source in open data and how to effectively deploy a holistic and sustainable open data initiative.

OSOD

OSOD gives government complete:

  • technical control over all internal open data efforts
  • freedom to fully engage with the open data community at large
