100% OPEN

A holistic, sustainable approach to government open data.

 

Mission

OSOD will bring open source awareness to the open data community and help educate all stakeholders on the importance of open source in open data and how to effectively deploy a holistic and sustainable open data initiative.

 
 

Why 100% OPEN?

100% OPEN gives governments complete:

control over all aspects of their internal open data efforts

freedom to fully engage with the open data community at large
